## Preparation

<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="HiHope AT">
    <Steps>
    1. Connect the HiHope HH-D03 development board to your computer, and open the NearLink ToolBox to check if it is properly connected.
    ![WeChat_20250104223851.png](https://s2.loli.net/2025/01/04/O7FhobzNpyiCAn9.jpg)
    2. On the firmware page, select **HiHope HH-D03 (BS21) AT Firmware**, click on it to find the file page, and then click **One-Click Burn**. After verifying the information, click **Confirm Burn**.
    ![WeChat_20250104224426.png](https://s2.loli.net/2025/01/04/HAVumdnbkhxRETy.jpg)
    3. After the process is complete, press the button on the board to burn the AT firmware. Once the burning is complete, you're done.
    4. Continue on the file page, find the button on the right side of the **HiHope HH-D03 Multi-Command Set Professional Edition.json** under the serial command set, click **One-Click Import**, and then click **Confirm Download**.
    ![WeChat_20250104224950.png](https://s2.loli.net/2025/01/04/SuQesGNFCmzIAlb.jpg)
    5. Once everything is ready, click on the **Serial Terminal** page, configure it, and then click connect. Test the **AT** command; if it returns OK, it is a preliminary success, as shown in the image:
    ![WeChat_20250105002543.png](https://s2.loli.net/2025/01/05/WMzDVGtK7hkgxTm.jpg)
    6. Please do not leave the current page of the software. Click to go to the [HiHope HH-D03 AT Usage](/at-guides/hihope-hhd03#hiHope-at-command-usage-tutorial) page to view detailed usage instructions.
    </Steps>
  </TabItem>
